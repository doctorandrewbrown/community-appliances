About us

Welcome to our community charity shop nestled in the heart of a quaint Welsh town! At our store, we're more than just a retail space; we're a hub of goodwill and sustainability. Our dedicated team of local volunteers works tirelessly to refurbish and safety-check donated appliances like washing machines, tumble dryers, and cookers. Our commitment to affordability ensures that everyone in our town has access to quality appliances without breaking the bank!
